🚀 Launch faster

Whether you are building an extension, designing a new feature or writing support copy, the Firefox Design System will help you get there faster. We help you drive greater efficiency through well-defined and reusable components.

🛠 Build easily

Well-articulated principles and well-documented standards make your decisions for building Firefox products easier.

🎨 Style recognizably

Our products are built to support a great deal of user control, but we still want them to look, feel and sound like they come from the same place and share the same spirit.

😍 Function enjoyably

Having a consistent appearance and voice builds credibility and trust for people to enjoy using Firefox products. With our products people feel more capable, well informed, and entertained.
