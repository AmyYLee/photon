Message bar

Message bars are used to communicate information to the user. The message bars show at the top of the page or section they apply to.

Usage

A message bars contain a one or more actions, neither of which may be Dismiss or Cancel.

Types

General

Message bars contain a concise and scannable text directly related to the action performed or to be performed. They may contain a text action. They always contain a icon before the message.

Dismissable

Unless they contain critical information, message bars are dismissible by clicking on the ghost button.

With buttons

Add one or two buttons in the Messabe bar when you want users to take an action after reading the message.

Anatomy

Size

Message bars have a fixed width and height may vary depending on the content. It cannot grow more than its parent container.

Title

In case the message you're trying to comunicate it's really complex, you can use a title. When possible, communicate the main message using just the title.

Message

The body of the message should be contained within two lines. Be descriptive and include any troubleshooting actions.

Dismissal

All notifications can be dismiss. Typically, it is a small “x” in the right corner.
