Illustrations

Firefox illustratons are approachable, supportive and whimsical.

Principles

Having principles helps to stay true to our visual language and with that to have a more cohesive experience in the Firefox products. Our illustration are approachanle, supportive and whimsical.

When to use it

Illustrations are a great and powerful tool. For this reason, you must be careful identify the right situation to use it. If the illustration is used only as a decorative element, it may loose its communicative power and distract from the general Firefox experience.

Use illustrations to help the user when is frustrated, or lost.

Use illustrations to reinforce a feature which is being misued, or neglected.

Use illustrations in heavy text component, it can help to explicit the content and encourage to read it.

Illustrations can be used to communicate differently with the users, especially when you did to congratulate or celebrate with them.

Where to use it

Illustrations must be used with parsimony and only in few context.

Error states

When something goes wrong but not when the user is danger.

On-boarding

When introducing a new feature, or how to use your product.

Long announcements

How to use it

Knowing where to use the right illustration according to the proper context should be done with forethought and attention.

Background colors

Firefox illustrations work better with the light grey background #f9f9fa.

Trasparency

Firefox illustrations are meant to be used always without opacity.

Customisation

It's not possuible to alter these illustrations in any way, or combine them with any other graphics, without the consent from Content Team.

Dimensions

Illustrations always keep the same ratio. The should take maximum 40% of the avaialable space, minumum 20%.
