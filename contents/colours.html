States

Firefox components usually have three different states: active, hover, and pressed. The hover colour is one step darker and the pressed colour is two steps darker.

Validation States

When you design a component that features validation use the following colour definitions for success, warning or error. Don't use the warning colour as text color because it fails WCAG 2.0.

Without background

This device had joined the party!

Fails WCAG 2.0

With background

Your Wi-Fi network has changed.

You must enter your age to sign up.
