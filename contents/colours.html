States

Firefox components usually have different states: active, hover, pressed, focused and disabled. Starting from the primary color set the hover state color one step darker in the color scale and the pressed state two steps darker.

Accesibility

Color contrast

Follow the WCAG 2.0 Level AA guidelines to ensure proper color contrast. Bodies of text should have a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 and that large-scale text should have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1.

Color blind

There are different types of color blindness. The most common form is reg-green color blidness, followed by blue-yellow color blindness and total color blindness. Red-green color blindness affects up to 8% of males and 0.5% of females. Ensure that adjacent color shades are blind-safe. In case of doubts use a color blindness analyser.

High contrast on Windows

Windows currently offers 4 high contrast modes: High Contrast #1, High Contrast #2, High Contrast Black and High Contrast White. Ensure to define colors also for this themes when you design a new feature.
