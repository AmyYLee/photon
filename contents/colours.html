States

Firefox components usually have three different states: active, hover, and pressed. The hover color is one step darker of your primary color and the pressed color is two steps darker.

Validation States

When you design a component that features validation use the following colors for success, warning or error. Don't use the warning yellow as text color because it does not ensure enough contrast.

Accesibility

Color contrast

Follow the WCAG 2.0 Level AA guidelines to ensure proper color contrast. Bodies of text should have a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 and that large-scale text should have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1.

Color blind

There are different types of color blindness. The most common form is reg-green color blidness, followed by blue-yellow color blindness and total color blindness. Red-green color blindness affects up to 8% of males and 0.5% of females. Ensure that between adjacent color shades are blind-safe. In case of doubts use a color blindness analyser in order to test your palette.
