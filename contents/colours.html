States

Firefox components usually have three different states: active, hover, and pressed. The hover color is one step darker and the pressed color is two steps darker.

Validation States

When you design a component that features validation use the following color definitions for success, warning or error. Don't use the warning color as text color because it fails WCAG 2.0.

Accesibility

Color contrast

Follow WCAG 2.0 Level AA guidelines in order to set the color contrast of your typography properly. Make sure that bodies of text have a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 and that large-scale text have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1.

Color blind

There are different types of color blindness: the most common form is reg-green color blidness, followed by blue-yellow color blindness and total color blindness. Red-green color blindness affects up to 8% of males and 0.5% of females. In case of doubts use a color blindness analyser in order to test if you color palette is blind-safe.
