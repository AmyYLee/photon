Typography

We believe good typography is invisible. We want for our users to have the most seamless experience while they're using Firefox products.

Color

Choose text color based on purpose. Primary for titles, buttons and most important bodies of text. Secondary for accompaingn text that gives further information, details or features to the primary content.
