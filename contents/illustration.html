Illustration

Firefox illustrations are approachable, supportive and whimsical.

Context

Illustrations are a visually and emotionally powerful tool. Therefore you should use them sparingly and carefully identify the right situations to use them. If an illustration is used only as a decorative element, it may loose its communicative power and distract people.

Error

Use illustration to encourage people that are frustrated, or lost. Especially when something has gone wrong. Do not use illustration when the user is in danger!

On-boarding

Use illustration to introduce and explain a product, or feature. This can be especially helpful for new, misused, or neglected features.

Announcement

Use illustration to help people understand content by exemplifying it. This is especially useful in text-heavy components like announcements.

Success

Use illustration to compliment people or celebrate with them.

How to use it

Knowing where to use the right illustration according to the proper context should be done with forethought and attention.

Background colors

Firefox illustration works better with the light grey background #f9f9fa.

Transparency

Firefox illustration is meant to be used always without opacity.

Customization

It's not possible to alter an illustration in any way, or combine it with any other graphics, without the consent of the Content Team.

Dimensions

Illustration always keeps the same ratio. The should take maximum 40% of the available space, minimum 20%.
